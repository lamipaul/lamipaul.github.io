---
title: "Fundamental frequency estimation"
---

Extracting frequency contours is a common challenge in bioacoustic analysis.

- For using a fundamental frequency (F0) estimator and tonal detector on your own signals, find this [python interface](https://github.com/mim-team/bioacoustic_F0_estimation).

- If you wish to test your own F0 estimor on various species, find this [benchmark dataset](https://doi.org/10.5061/dryad.prr4xgxw8).

- If you want to read details on the dataset or the F0 estimator, find the [associated publication](https://doi.org/10.1080/09524622.2025.2500380).
