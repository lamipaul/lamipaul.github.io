---
title: "Vocalisation clustering based on deep embeddings"
---

Animal vocalisations typically fall into discrete categories, whether of individual signatures or of call types.

- For using a vocalisation clustering model on your own signals, find this [python interface](https://gitlab.lis-lab.fr/paul.best/repertoire_embedder).

- If you wish to test your own F0 estimor on various species, find this [benchmark dataset](https://doi.org/10.6084/m9.figshare.23138210.v1).

- If you want to read details on the dataset or the clutering model, find the [associated publication](https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pone.0283396).
