---
title: "Multimodal interaction analysis"
---

Analysing structure and patterns in multimodal interactions is challenging because various signals often overlap.
[Here](https://osf.io/zdruq/overview) is a tool to help you overcom this challenge, based on behavioural annotations from Boris or Elan.
To check the implementation details and motivation for these analyses, find the [associated publication](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.anbehav.2026.123526)
